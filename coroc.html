Crocodile Ranch

Take a break from relaxing by the pool at the lodge to watch crocodiles relaxing by the pool. The Crocodile Ranch was established in 1984 by the government of Ethiopia to sustainably maintain the wild croc population. It lies on an area of 3 hectare of land. Feeding usually happens on Mondays and Thursdays and is not to be missed.
